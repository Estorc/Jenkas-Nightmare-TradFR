About the Walkthrough


The included walkthrough is written as an attempt to help players through the game, while trying to be as spoiler-free as possible. Players should be aware that the "Collectables" and "Secrets" sections of the walkthrough are understandably ignorant of this statement.

Many pages will be introduced with text in GREEN, for advice regarding the information that follows it.

Pages with a heading marked in RED text will contain very blatant spoilers.

Many pages in the walkthrough itself may forego using red text, and instead cover these spoilers with black boxes. Hovering your mouse over these black sections will reveal the text hidden underneath it. These types of spoilers will likely hide the solutions to puzzles, or special strategies in dealing with the more difficult bosses in the game.
