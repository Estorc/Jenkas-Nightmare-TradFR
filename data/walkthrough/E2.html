NG+ Weapons and Capsules


All NG+ weapons and capsule openers, and the capsule contents, are listed below.
The Nemesis

The ●NEMESIS● is the only weapon exclusive to NG+, and requires a bit of set-up. In your original profile, make sure you pick up the Little Man from the Cemetery, and you can either return him home or keep him with you. Then, in Jenka's Nightmare, head to the Conduit, and carefully make your way DOWN to another door.
Inside is the Little's Cottage. This time around, you can trade any weapon you like with the Little Man in exchange for the ●NEMESIS●.
Snake Capsule

After you obtain the ●CRITTER JUICE● from Chaba's storage, return to Chaba and speak with him a few times. If you have the ●SNAKE CAPSULE● with you, he'll offer to open it for you.
Reward: The ●HYDRA● - A version of the ●SNAKE● with unlimited ammo.
Polar Star Capsule

While in possession of the ●POLAR STAR CAPSULE●, the events with the Gunsmith will change to a more cynical version of himself. After speaking with him and completing the events for the ●SPUR●, he'll open this capsule for you as an upgrade to the ●SPUR● itself.
Reward: The ●URSA MINOR● - Your ●SPUR● will charge twice as fast.
Machine Gun Capsule

After both Genesis and Oblivion are defeated, meet up with Curly, either at the Town Hall, or Observatory. Speak with her a few times, and she'll offer to open the ●MACHINE GUN CAPSULE● for you.
Reward: The ●TURBOCHARGE● - Your ●MACHINE GUN● will recharge much faster.
Missile Launcher Capsule

Speak with Balrog a few times either at the Core, or after meeting with him in the Observatory. He'll offer to open this capsule for you. Its contents will vary depending on which missile launcher you have with you at the time.
Reward: +30/+10 ●MISSILE LAUNCHER●/●SUPER MISSILE● ammo.
Bubbler Capsule

Finish the events at the Sanctuary, and speak with Jack. After the additional dialogue from obtaining ●EXCALIBUR●, speak with him again and he'll offer to open this capsule.
Reward: Life Capsule (+4 or +5, depending on other NG+ Capsules).
Super Missiles Capsule

Speak with Balrog a few times either at the Core, or after meeting with him in the Observatory. He'll offer to open this capsule for you. Its contents will vary depending on which missile launcher you have with you at the time.
Reward: +9/+3 ●MISSILE LAUNCHER●/●SUPER MISSILE● ammo.
Spur Capsule

Speak with the Gunsmith a few times. He'll offer to open this capsule for you, even before you have him complete the ●SPUR●.
Reward: The ●MUNITIONS CLIP● - Use it to restore all ammo, instantly!
